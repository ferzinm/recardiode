THE PURSUE FITNESS WAY

No Hidden Fees

Why should you have to pay a high enrollment fee to sign up for a gym membership? Pursue Fitness is an affordable gym with a one time only low enrollment fee, low annual fee, and no other hidden fees.  We try to keep our gym memberships affordable to all.

No Contracts

Not sure how long your affordable gym membership will last? Don’t worry. Pursue Fitness is month to month with no contracts. We know you are going to love our gym; however, life happens! If you want to change your membership plan or cancel your gym membership you may do so at any time with no fees.

Fantastic Group
Fitness Classes

Let’s face it; sometimes working out by your self is difficult. A group fitness class is a fun way to stay fit, meet new friends, and challenge yourself physically all while having a great time!

Tanning and
Massage Chairs

Pursue Fitness is the gym for you! No high priced tanning salon here. For just $25 monthly a Plus gym membership gives you access to our high quality tanning beds and luxurious massage chairs.

Personal or
Group Training

Pursue Fitness is a gym that prides itself on top quality training. Whether you want one on one personal training, team training, or a high intensity style, Pursue Fitness has you covered with affordable pricing.
